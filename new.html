DREAM SPACE SIMULATOR (NASA international TOP 5 project)
May 2017

Won the local People Choice award and went on to represent INDIA internationally in the GLOBAL top 5 out of 2000+ international projects. The project was a complete simulator with a feedback chair and an interactive glove. User could experience launch and a virtual replication of a space mission. The VR provided a 3D experience in which users could interact using the glove. The complete KIT was controlled using an AI BOT which by using relays could communicate with the chair and VR upon giving linguistic commands.

Automatic light sensing using fuzzy logic
July 2016 - November 2016

The project is a small prototype which makes use of the distance of a person from the light source and according to the proximity the light intensity brightens or dims. The light which would have been switched on under normal conditions will be dimmed in this model saving energy and reducing the cost.
